Delightful Nut Roast Recipe

Recipe Details

This recipe looks at the combination of all types of nuts to put in your mouth

Ingredients

  • nuts
  • More nuts
  • Even more nuts
  • Butter or something

Steps:

  1. Blend or grind nuts together
  2. Mix with butter
  3. Put into a oven proof dish
  4. Preheat oven then somehow put in right after if your oven is amazing.
